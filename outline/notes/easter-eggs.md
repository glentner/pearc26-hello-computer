## Notes on Usage

- References should be recognizable but not require knowledge to understand the paper
- The section content should make sense even if reader doesn't get the reference
- References span 1968-1999 (classic sci-fi era) - intentional nod to foundational works
- Mix of film and TV, serious and comedic
- Douglas Adams appears twice (Mostly Harmless, 42) - his sensibility fits the tone
